K R A M P U S

A Folklore Tale

Krampus is a story told to kids, like Santa. Only difference is, Krampus is Santas Evil Twin, told to kids to keep them from misbehaving. It is said he appears and steals kids, chaining them up and carting them off to hell. His Job is to punish the "Naughty" children.
